Our aim is to provide an interactive and responsive website to educate children in an innovative way about the elements present in periodic table.
Details such as their physical properties, chemical properties and their electronic configuration can be annotated through interesting animation or graphics.
Also categorization of elements on basis of similar properties helps to remember the elements in an effecient manner.
Elementos is an alternative to other existing methods to teach children about the periodic table and properties of elements of the periodic table.
It also features a smart interactive mode which is always ready to solve your queries related to periodic table and physical properties, chemical properties and their electronic configuration of the elements.
Mini games helps to keep young learners excited, involved and enthusiastic for learning more.
One of the interesting activity includes a mini game that allows children to identify the position of a particular element from a masked periodic table.
This would help children to have a better understanding about the positioning of elements in periodic table.
The use of mnemonics provide an easy way to memorise the elements that are placed along periods and groups in periodic table.
Also students are made aware of valuable contribution made by some Indian Chemists in the field of Chemistry.
